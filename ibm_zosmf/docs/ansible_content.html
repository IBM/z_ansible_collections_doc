IBM z/OSMF¶

The IBM z/OS Management Facility (z/OSMF) collection, also represented as ibm_zosmf in this document, is part of the broader initiative to bring Ansible Automation to IBM Z® through the offering Red Hat® Ansible Certified Content for IBM Z.

The IBM z/OSMF collection supports automation tasks such as operating z/OS workflows, provisioning and managing z/OS middlewares/softwares, via z/OSMF RESTful services.
