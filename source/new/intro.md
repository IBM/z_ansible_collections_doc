# Introduction to Ansible for IBM Z

Ansible for IBM Z extends the power of automation from Red Hat Ansible to IBM Z environments, enabling efficient and consistent management across hybrid infrastructures. With specialized modules and seamless integration, Ansible for IBM Z can help you simplify and streamline many z/OS operations, for example:
    - Managing z/OS data sets
    - Submitting z/OS jobs
    - Provisioning and de-provisiong users

Ansible content is distributed in the form of collections, which includes playbooks, roles, modules, and plugins. IBM provides several collections for different IBM Z offerings, and you can work with them to automate different tasks for your own needs.
